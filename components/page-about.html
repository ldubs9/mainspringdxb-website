Our Story
"At Mainspring, I don't just sell watches — I match people with stories they want to wear." — Erkan, Founder & Personal Watch Collector

Watches are my passion, my hobby. To keep that passion alive — to extend my collection, buy new pieces, and fund their maintenance — I began using my own collection as a source. Over time, somehow, it turned into a business as well.

When it comes to buying a watch, I am just like you: asking, asking, asking. Checking every detail, every part, cross-referencing, verifying, negotiating — and beyond all of that, searching for the story, the character behind each piece before deciding to buy.

I have a small but highly trusted network of suppliers and experienced collectors I source from. Beyond my own inspection expertise, I always obtain a warranty from my suppliers. That is how I can offer an authenticity warranty on every watch, plus a three-month movement warranty on each piece.

On your behalf, I ensure the watch I — and therefore you — buy is the real thing. That is why I call myself your personal watch collector.
