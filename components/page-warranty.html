Our Policies

Warranty & Refund Policy

Our specific policy for second-hand and vintage watches — please read carefully before purchasing.

Our Commitment to You
At Mainspring we collect watches not just for how they look or tick, but for what they represent. We specialize in authentic watches with character and history. Each piece is carefully sourced and personally inspected. We do not — and will never — sell counterfeits.

Condition & Expectations

All watches sold by us are second-hand, and mostly vintage. They are not new. As such, expect minor signs of wear, patina, or imperfections consistent with their age. We describe each watch accurately and provide detailed photographs for every listing.

By purchasing from us, you accept the watch's vintage condition, its cosmetic appearance, and any wear marks detailed in the product description. The history and uniqueness of these watches are part of their charm — perfection is not the point.

Our 3-Month Limited Warranty

Unlike most second-hand watch sellers, we provide a specific limited warranty covering core functional components:

  • Coverage (3 months from purchase): Strictly limited to the internal movement and proper battery function for quartz watches.
  • Movement failure: If a defect is found, we will first attempt to repair the movement, or replace the watch with an identical model if available.
  • Dead battery: If the battery fails within three months, we replace it free of charge.
  • Timekeeping adjustment: Vintage mechanical watches are never perfectly accurate. If yours runs fast or slow, we will adjust it free of charge within three months.
  • Unresolved movement issue: If the same problem recurs after our repair attempt, you may request a full refund of the purchase price.

What Is Not Covered

  1. External components: Case, crystal, dial, hands, strap, bracelet, lugs, bezel, crown, and winding/setting mechanisms are not covered — these are subject to wear and user interaction.
  2. Water resistance: All watches are sold as not water-resistant. Vintage seals degrade over time. Any moisture or submersion damage is entirely at the owner's risk and voids all warranty claims.
  3. Accidental damage / misuse: Physical shock, misuse, neglect, unauthorized repairs, or accidental damage void all warranty claims.

Refund & Exchange Policy

  • No change-of-mind refunds: We do not refund if you simply change your mind, provided the watch is working as described. However, you may exchange it for any other watch in the collection within three months, provided it is in the same condition as when sold.
  • Refund for unfixable movement issues: A refund is only issued if we fail to repair a warranted movement defect after our initial attempt.
  • Exchange option (within 3 months): Trade in your watch toward any other piece in our inventory. You pay the difference if the new watch costs more. Valid once per purchase.

Compliance Disclaimer

These warranty terms are our own business policy for second-hand items, consistent with standard industry practices for vintage sales. By completing your purchase, you acknowledge and agree to all terms above.
