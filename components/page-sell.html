The Process

Why Our Offers Are Below Market

Mainspring is a licensed company with real operating costs: rent, licences, staff, marketing, servicing, warranty obligations, and capital lock-up. We typically buy in lots — not one-by-one — so our average sourcing cost must stay low to keep our prices competitive and our services sustainable.

Any cash offer will be significantly below final market value. This is normal and unavoidable in the professional watch trade. If this doesn't suit you and you're not in a rush, we'll honestly advise you to sell privately instead.

Inspection Is Mandatory

Before any offer, we perform a physical and movement inspection using professional tools and expertise. All watches submitted are subject to an inspection fee (depending on brand, model, and complexity), paid upfront and non-refundable.

The fee covers authentication, condition assessment, and market evaluation — and includes a written authenticity/condition report with a Mainspring certificate. The inspection fee may be deducted from the final price if a deal is completed.

Your Options

Option A — Direct Sale

We make a wholesale cash offer — below market price. You're under no obligation to accept. If you do, payment is made within 10 working days via the agreed method, against a legal receipt.

Option B — Consignment

Leave the watch with us for an agreed period (30+ days). We market and sell it on your behalf and pay you the net agreed price once sold — typically a higher return, in exchange for time and patience. A written agreement defines price, commission, and duration.

Legal Requirements

To sell or consign a watch you must be the legal owner, provide valid ID, and confirm the watch is not stolen, under dispute, or under any finance or insurance claim. We reserve the right to refuse any transaction, and will hold and report any counterfeit items to UAE authorities.

Our Promise

Honest advice — even if that means telling you not to sell to us. Professional inspection standards. Clear, market-based offers. No pressure. Respect for your time and your timepiece.
