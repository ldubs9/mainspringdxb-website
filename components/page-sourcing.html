How It Works

1 Scope of Service

We can source any watch brand, model, or reference — subject to market availability — covering vintage, neo-vintage, and modern pre-owned watches, through our professional dealer network, private collectors, and international partners.

2 How to Start a Request

To begin, provide at least one of the following:

  • Exact reference number
  • Clear photos of the desired watch
  • Detailed description — brand, model, size, dial, bracelet/strap, metal, year range, etc.
The more specific your request, the faster the sourcing, the better the pricing, and the more accurate the match. Specificity signals serious intent and allows us to work more efficiently on your behalf.

3 Deposit — 25%

Once your target watch is confirmed with a realistic market price range, a deposit of 25% of the average market value is required to activate sourcing.

The deposit is non-refundable but fully redeemable — toward the sourced watch or any other watch purchased from Mainspring in the future. If you cancel, the deposit remains as store credit. No cash refunds will be issued.

4 Timeline

Within 10 calendar days of receiving the deposit, we present 1–3 suitable options. If none are approved, a further 1–3 options follow within the next 10 days.

If both rounds are declined, additional sourcing rounds require a further 25% deposit under the same non-refundable, redeemable terms.

5 Viewing

Once you select a preferred option, we arrange a personal viewing. Viewings are conducted one watch at a time to ensure focused inspection and professional handling of each piece.

6 Authentication & Certificate

Upon your approval, we perform full authenticity checks: case inspection, dial/hands/crown/components, movement inspection, and serial/reference verification. We then issue an official stamped and signed Certificate of Authenticity and accept full legal liability for the watch's authenticity.

7 Warranty, Invoice & VAT

All sourced watches include a 3-month movement warranty under our standard warranty terms. Upon purchase you receive an official Mainspring invoice inclusive of UAE VAT.
